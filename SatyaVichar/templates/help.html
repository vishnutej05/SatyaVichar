Adani Group acquires majority stake in news agency IANS

Adani Group said on Friday that AMG Media Networks Limited (AMNL), a wholly owned subsidiary of the company, has acquired a 50.50 per cent stake in news agency IANS India Private Limited. This majority stake constitutes Equity Shares (Category I shares - with voting rights) and Equity Shares (Category II shares - without voting rights) each of IANS India Private Limited (IANS) in accordance with Share Purchase Agreement dated December 15, 2023, the company announced through an exchange filing.
